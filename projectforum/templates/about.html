About Project Forum

The purpose of ProjectForum is to bring people together: Those with ideas but in need of someone with know-how to make them real, and those with experience looking to sharpen their skills. It is the product of a semester-long project in the CS169 course at UC Berkeley.

This project was put together by Alex, Emily, Sarah, and Cedric. You can contribute to the ProjectForum or report a bug via github.
